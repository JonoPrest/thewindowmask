About

About

The Window Mask was created by Rowan Banks, an occupational therapist with a passion for sewing and creating things that are both beautiful and useful.


During 2020 we all had to start wearing face masks. Her cousin, Megan, mentioned to Rowan how hard it was for her to hear what people were saying as she couldn’t see their lips moving. Megan was born with Waardenburg Syndrome which causes hearing loss.


Using online resources and some of her own adaptations, Rowan created a window mask for Megan. They were blown away by the interest, support and encouragement they received.


"It has been a beautiful experience to hear the stories of how the Window Mask is making it possible for people from all walks of life to be able to communicate effectively in their home, work and social lives."


Rowan partnered with two highly skilled and innovative women, Deeva van der Merwe, the director of Merwe Mode production house, and Christina Botha. Together they are striving to produce a top quality Window Mask that makes it possible for everyone to truly connect with their family, friends, colleagues and clients.

A face mask with a difference! The Window Mask allows you to show your smile, and communicate effectively while still remaining protected. Originally designed to assist with lip reading for the Deaf community, and now available to order.

  • Double layer of 100% cotton fabric
  • Durable plastic window with UV protection
  • Hand wash or gentle machine wash
  • Option to wear with elastic loops or ties

To prevent misting, rub a small amount of dishwashing liquid, shaving cream, coconut oil, olive oil or toothpaste on the inside of the plastic window.

Sizes:

  • Small - Suitable for children age 8-13, and teens/adults with small faces.
  • Medium - Suitable for most teens and adults (pictured above).
  • Large - Suitable for adults with broader faces or those who prefer a looser fit.

Our Window Masks are R120 each, with discounts available on sets of more than three. Delivery is available across South Africa and internationally. Contact us below to find out more or to order.
